SCHOOLS

Here you can find some of NZ Latin Dance schools

Viva Dance

Auckland

We have classes that are perfect for both the complete beginner looking to just pick up some basic steps for social dancing, and for more serious dancers wanting to improve their skills on the dance floor or on the performance stage. You get the best quality of teaching at Viva Dance as all our teachers are highly trained dancers that are carefully chosen for their talent, enthusiasm and professionalism.

RYDE

Auckland

Ricardo and Yan have performed and competed all around the world, and now bring their enthusiasm for performance and competition to Auckland. As well as being invited around New Zealand to teach, they run technique focussed classes where solid fundamentals are taught right from the beginning, with a variety of moves and shines. They also run competition and performance teams in a fun and friendly envrionment.
Highly energetic, they push their dancers to be well rounded in all elements of salsa, from understanding of music and different styles of salsa, to social dancing, performing, and competing. They encourage everyone to grow and learn together in a community.

Salsa con Coco

Auckland

Jorge (Coco) Sequeiros was born in Peru, South America and has been living in New Zealand since November 1991 and dancing Salsa for as long as he can remember. Salsa being a passion for him wasn’t enough so it also became his way of life, a way of life which he is trying to share with New Zealanders so that they also will know the passion of the dancing, music, food and culture of his homeland. Coco considers his Salsa style to be a Kiwi style originally derived from a combination of the Salsa of Peru and the Salsa danced in New Zealand!

Passion of Expression

Auckland

Passion of Expression, Andrada & Alberto are ready to teach you some new and exciting Salsa and Bachata classes. We’re looking forward to catching up with everyone in class. Our dance school Passion of Expression will introduce you to the basics of Bachata and Salsa, a Caribbean style of dance and then teach you a turn pattern to try out on the dance floor with a partner.

Studio 7

Auckland

Studio 7 combines some of the top professional performers and instructors in New Zealand. Come and learn how to dance in a fun social environment!

Sweet Azucar!

Auckland

Our classes in West Auckland aim to provide new and more experienced dancers with great technique and awesome choreographies, all in a fun and safe environment. We are passionate about Latin Dance and we truly believe dance is for everyone!

ZoukArt - Trajano & Shannon

Auckland

Zouk Art started as a brand where we could combine both our artistic and creative natures into the dance, Brazilian Zouk. It has now become a dance school specialising in Brazilian Dances, both Zouk and Samba de Gafieira - where we are dedicated to a syllabus that will teach you solid fundamentals from the ground up so that any student keen to learn how to dance CAN. We teach classes, workshops, run performance teams, can provide ‘dance privates’ and we also choreograph our own performances. We love to work with all styles of dance, different schools and are always open to new and exciting opportunities.

The Cuban Groove

Auckland

Vivio teaches, performs and choreographs under the name The Cuban Groove Dance School. Whether you join a class, or witness a live performance, you'll be captivated by his magical presence on the dance floor, and fun-loving approach to teaching.

Dança - The Home of Kizomba in Wellington

Wellington

Dancing Kizomba is a very unique experience. The idea is two people moving as one. Try the Free Intro and discover the magic of connection that is Kizomba!

Dance Whanganui

Whanganui

We offer dance classes in many styles for adults and children. We offer not only Street Latin, but also jazz, burlesque, ballet workout, fitness classes and custom choreography for couples.

The Dance Factory NZ

Tauranga & Mount Maunganui

We are based in the Waikato & Bay of Plenty regions and seek to grow classes throughout New Zealand. We run weekly partner dance classes teaching various styles of Zouk, Samba de Gafieira, Brazilian Salsa and Bachata from beginner level to intermediate/advanced. We host a number of events and workshops in the region for learning and fun. We can provide private lessons for groups or couples for your special event.

Bay Salsa

Tauranga

Bay Salsa is a community based club that promotes and fosters street Latin dance in the Bay of Plenty. We are a group of people who enjoy salsa socially and run a number of parties through the year. Bay Salsa run classes in bachata, salsa and zouk in Tauranga. It is also home to the Bay Salsa Festival, which seeks to support New Zealand Salsa by providing a stage to grow and develop as well as financial grants for those teams representing New Zealand on the world stage.

Latin Dance

Tauranga

Come and learn to Latin dance with us! Our classes are a great way to meet people, learn some new moves, and keep fit and active! We have dance classes in the latin dance styles of Salsa, Bachata, Samba de Gafieira, Zouk Lambada and now Argentine Tango.

Salsa ¡Agua!

Tauranga

Kate, the main instructor and Director of Salsa ¡Agua!, has been dancing Latin dance for more than 20 years. She has been teaching Cuban dance and Salsa since 2008. Her patient, fun, energetic teaching style is contagious and she will have you dancing and having a great time before you know it.

Pasion Salsa

Cromwell, Queenstown and Wanaka

Pasión Salsa offers Salsa and Bachata classes in Cromwell, Queenstown and Wanaka. Everyone is welcome to come and learn how to dance with us!
